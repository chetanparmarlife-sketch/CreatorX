## Getting Started

### What is CreatorX?

CreatorX is a platform that connects creators with brands for influencer marketing campaigns. As a creator, you can:

- Discover campaigns that match your niche
- Apply to campaigns
- Submit deliverables
- Earn money
- Communicate with brands

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Download the App

1. Download CreatorX from the App Store or Google Play
2. Open the app

### Step 2: Sign Up

1. Tap "Sign Up"
2. Select "I'm a Creator"
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Enter your name
6. Enter your phone number (optional)
7. Tap "Create Account"

### Step 3: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email inbox
2. Click the verification link
3. Return to the app
4. Your email is now verified!

## Completing Your Profile

### Step 1: Add Profile Picture

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Tap on your profile picture
3. Choose "Take Photo" or "Choose from Gallery"
4. Crop and adjust your photo
5. Tap "Save"

**Tips**:
- Use a clear, professional photo
- Make sure your face is visible
- Use good lighting

### Step 2: Add Bio

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Tap "Edit Profile"
3. Enter your bio (max 500 characters)
4. Describe your content niche, style, and what makes you unique
5. Tap "Save"

**Example Bio**:
```
Fashion & lifestyle content creator | 50K+ followers
📍 Mumbai | ✈️ Travel enthusiast
Creating content that inspires ✨
```

### Step 3: Add Portfolio Items

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Tap "Portfolio"
3. Tap "Add Item"
4. Upload photos or videos of your best work
5. Add a description for each item
6. Tap "Save"

**Tips**:
- Showcase your best work
- Include variety (photos, videos, reels)
- Add descriptions explaining the campaign/brand

### Step 4: Set Your Niche

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Tap "Edit Creator Profile"
3. Select your niche (Fashion, Food, Travel, etc.)
4. Enter your follower count
5. Enter your engagement rate (if known)
6. Tap "Save"

## Verifying Your Identity

### Why KYC is Required

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is required to:
- Withdraw your earnings
- Build trust with brands
- Comply with financial regulations

### Step 1: Start KYC Verification

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Tap "KYC Verification"
3. Tap "Start Verification"

### Step 2: Upload Documents

You'll need to upload:

1. **Government ID** (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, or Driver's License)
   - Take a clear photo of the front
   - Take a clear photo of the back (if applicable)
   - Make sure all text is readable

2. **Address Proof** (Utility bill, Bank statement, etc.)
   - Must be recent (within 3 months)
   - Must show your name and address

3. **Selfie** (for identity verification)
   - Hold your ID next to your face
   - Make sure both are clearly visible

### Step 3: Submit for Review

1. Review all uploaded documents
2. Make sure everything is clear and readable
3. Tap "Submit for Review"
4. Wait for verification (usually 24-48 hours)

### Step 4: Check Status

1. Go to Profile tab
2. Tap "KYC Verification"
3. Check your verification status:
   - **Pending**: Under review
   - **Verified**: Approved! You can now withdraw
   - **Rejected**: Review rejection reason and resubmit

## Setting Up Your Wallet

### Step 1: View Your Wallet

1. Go to Wallet tab
2. You'll see:
   - **Available Balance**: Money you can withdraw
   - **Pending Balance**: Money awaiting approval
   - **Total Earned**: All-time earnings

### Step 2: Add Bank Account

1. Go to Wallet tab
2. Tap "Bank Accounts"
3. Tap "Add Bank Account"
4. Enter your details:
   - Account Holder Name
   - Account Number
   - IFSC Code
   - Bank Name
   - Branch Name
   - UPI ID (optional)
5. Tap "Save"

**Important**:
- Account holder name must match your KYC documents
- Double-check your account number and IFSC code
- You can add multiple bank accounts

### Step 3: Set Default Account

1. Go to Wallet tab
2. Tap "Bank Accounts"
3. Tap the account you want to set as default
4. Tap "Set as Default"

## Next Steps

### 1. Browse Campaigns

1. Go to Explore tab
2. Browse available campaigns
3. Use filters to find campaigns that match your niche
4. Read campaign details carefully

### 2. Apply to Campaigns

1. Find a campaign you're interested in
2. Tap on the campaign
3. Read all requirements
4. Tap "Apply"
5. Write a compelling pitch
6. Submit your application

### 3. Track Your Applications

1. Go to Applications tab
2. View all your applications
3. Check status updates
4. Respond to brand messages

### 4. Submit Deliverables

1. When selected, you'll receive a notification
2. Go to Deliverables tab
3. View required deliverables
4. Upload your content
5. Submit for review

### 5. Earn and Withdraw

1. Once deliverables are approved, you'll receive payment
2. Go to Wallet tab
3. Check your balance
4. Request withdrawal
5. Money will be transferred to your bank account
