## Overview

### Wallet Balance Types

Your wallet shows three types of balances:

- **Available Balance**: Money you can withdraw immediately
- **Pending Balance**: Money awaiting approval (from completed campaigns)
- **Total Earned**: All-time earnings from all campaigns

### Withdrawal Process

1. Complete KYC verification
2. Add bank account
3. Request withdrawal
4. Wait for processing (2-5 business days)
5. Money transferred to your bank account

## Prerequisites

### 1. KYC Verification Required

**You must complete KYC verification before withdrawing.**

1. Go to **Profile** tab
2. Tap **"KYC Verification"**
3. Upload required documents:
   - Government ID
   - Address proof
   - Selfie
4. Wait for verification (24-48 hours)
5. Once verified, you can withdraw

**Why KYC?**
- Required by financial regulations
- Protects against fraud
- Enables secure payments

### 2. Minimum Balance

- **Minimum Withdrawal**: ₹100
- Check your available balance before requesting

## Viewing Your Balance

### Step 1: Open Wallet

1. Open CreatorX app
2. Go to **Wallet** tab
3. View your balances at the top

### Step 2: Understand Balances

- **Available Balance** (Green):
  - Money ready to withdraw
  - From approved deliverables
  - Can withdraw immediately

- **Pending Balance** (Yellow):
  - Money awaiting approval
  - From submitted deliverables
  - Will move to available after approval

- **Total Earned** (Blue):
  - All-time earnings
  - Includes withdrawn amounts
  - For reference only

### Step 3: View Transactions

1. Scroll down to see transactions
2. Tap on a transaction for details
3. See:
   - Transaction type (EARNING, WITHDRAWAL)
   - Amount
   - Date
   - Status
   - Related campaign

## Adding a Bank Account

### Step 1: Access Bank Accounts

1. Go to **Wallet** tab
2. Tap **"Bank Accounts"**
3. Tap **"Add Bank Account"**

### Step 2: Enter Details

Fill in all required information:

1. **Account Holder Name**:
   - Must match your KYC documents
   - Use full name as on ID

2. **Account Number**:
   - Your bank account number
   - Double-check for accuracy

3. **IFSC Code**:
   - 11-character code
   - Format: XXXX0XXXXX
   - Find on cheque or bank statement

4. **Bank Name**:
   - Select from dropdown or enter
   - Full bank name

5. **Branch Name**:
   - Branch where account is held
   - Optional but recommended

6. **UPI ID** (Optional):
   - For faster transfers
   - Format: name@bank

### Step 3: Verify and Save

1. **Review All Details**:
   - Check account number
   - Verify IFSC code
   - Ensure name matches KYC

2. **Save**:
   - Tap **"Save"**
   - Account is added
   - Can set as default

### Step 4: Set Default Account

1. Go to **Bank Accounts**
2. Tap on an account
3. Tap **"Set as Default"**
4. Default account used for withdrawals

**Tips**:
- ✅ Add multiple accounts if needed
- ✅ Keep one as default
- ✅ Update if account details change

## Requesting a Withdrawal

### Step 1: Check Prerequisites

Before requesting withdrawal:

- ✅ KYC is verified
- ✅ Bank account is added
- ✅ Available balance ≥ ₹100
- ✅ No pending issues

### Step 2: Start Withdrawal

1. Go to **Wallet** tab
2. Tap **"Withdraw"** button
3. Withdrawal form opens

### Step 3: Enter Amount

1. **Enter Amount**:
   - Minimum: ₹100
   - Maximum: Your available balance
   - Cannot exceed available balance

2. **Check Balance**:
   - Available balance shown
   - Amount you can withdraw
   - After withdrawal, new balance shown

### Step 4: Select Bank Account

1. **Choose Account**:
   - Select from your bank accounts
   - Default account pre-selected
   - Can change if needed

2. **Verify Account**:
   - Check account number
   - Verify IFSC code
   - Ensure it's correct

### Step 5: Review and Submit

1. **Review Details**:
   - Withdrawal amount
   - Bank account
   - Processing time (2-5 business days)

2. **Submit**:
   - Tap **"Request Withdrawal"**
   - Confirm withdrawal
   - Request submitted

### Step 6: Confirmation

After submitting:
- ✅ Withdrawal request created
- ✅ Status: PENDING
- ✅ Available balance reduced
- ✅ Pending withdrawal shown
- ✅ You'll receive confirmation

## Tracking Withdrawals

### View Withdrawal History

1. Go to **Wallet** tab
2. Tap **"Withdrawals"**
3. See all withdrawal requests

### Withdrawal Status

- **PENDING**:
  - Request submitted
  - Under processing
  - Usually 2-5 business days

- **PROCESSING**:
  - Payment being processed
  - Transfer initiated
  - Should complete soon

- **COMPLETED**:
  - Money transferred
  - Check your bank account
  - Transaction complete

- **FAILED**:
  - Transfer failed
  - Check bank details
  - Contact support

- **CANCELLED**:
  - You cancelled request
  - Money returned to wallet
  - Can request again

### Withdrawal Details

Tap on a withdrawal to see:
- Request date
- Amount
- Bank account
- Status
- Processing time
- Transaction ID (when completed)

### Cancel Withdrawal

If withdrawal is still PENDING:
1. Go to **Withdrawals**
2. Tap on withdrawal
3. Tap **"Cancel"**
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Money returned to wallet

## Common Questions

### How long does withdrawal take?

Usually **2-5 business days** from request to bank transfer. Processing time may vary.

### Is there a withdrawal fee?

No, CreatorX doesn't charge withdrawal fees. Your bank may charge minimal fees.

### What's the minimum withdrawal?

**₹100** is the minimum withdrawal amount.

### Can I withdraw pending balance?

No, only **available balance** can be withdrawn. Pending balance becomes available after deliverables are approved.

### What if withdrawal fails?

1. Check bank account details
2. Verify IFSC code
3. Contact support
4. Money will be returned to wallet

### Can I cancel a withdrawal?

Yes, if status is still **PENDING**. Once processing starts, cannot cancel.

### How many withdrawals can I make?

No limit on number of withdrawals. Each withdrawal must meet minimum amount (₹100).

### When will I receive payment?

- After deliverables are **approved**
- Balance moves from pending to available
- Then you can withdraw

### What if my bank account details are wrong?

1. Update bank account details
2. Contact support immediately
3. Withdrawal may fail if details incorrect
4. Money will be returned to wallet
