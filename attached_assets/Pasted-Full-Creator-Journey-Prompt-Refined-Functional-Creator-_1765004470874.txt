Full Creator Journey Prompt (Refined & Functional)

Creator Authentication Flow:

On the creator auth screen, display 3 centered social login buttons:

Instagram (official gradient background + white logo + text “Instagram”)

YouTube (official red background + white logo + text “YouTube”)

LinkedIn (official blue background + white logo + text “LinkedIn”)

Do not use “Continue with”. Use only platform names.

After connecting a platform, automatically fetch the creator’s follower count.

If the creator has ≥ 1000 followers, redirect them to the Profile Creation Screen.

If <1000 followers, show a blocking message: “You’re not eligible for collaborations yet.”

Creator Profile Creation Flow:

Creator fills: name, bio, niche, city, audience stats, pricing, sample content.

Creator dashboard unlocks after completion.

Campaign Discovery & Application Flow:

Dashboard shows available brand campaigns with filters (budget, category, platform).

Creator opens a campaign to see:

Campaign brief

Mandatory deliverables

Timeline

Requirements

Compensation

Creator taps “Apply to Campaign”.

Creator submits:

Short pitch

Expected timeline

Any extra details requested by brand

Brand Review Flow:

After applying, application status shows in creator dashboard:

Pending Review

Approved

Rejected

Brand sees the creator’s application in the brand dashboard with details, stats, pitch, pricing.

Brand can:

Approve

Reject

Request more info

After Approval – Deliverables Flow:

Once approved, creator sees the campaign moved to “Active Campaigns”.

Creator sees required deliverables and uploads:

Content drafts

Thumbnail options

Captions & hashtags

Raw files (if required)

Deliverable status options:

Draft Submitted

Brand Reviewing

Changes Requested

Approved

Posted (with link)

Brand Feedback Flow:

Brand reviews submitted content.

Brand can:

Request revisions with comments

Approve final content

Once approved, creator posts content with required hashtags & tags.

Creator submits post URL or screenshot for verification.

Campaign Completion Flow:

After final approval + posting proof, campaign moves to Completed.

Payment status shows:

Pending

Processing

Paid

Creator can rate the brand.

Brand can rate the creator.

End-to-End Functionality Summary

This flow includes:
✔ Creator social auth
✔ Eligibility check
✔ Profile creation
✔ Campaign discovery
✔ Application + status
✔ Brand side review
✔ Deliverables submission
✔ Feedback + approvals
✔ Posting verification
✔ Payment tracking
✔ Ratings & completion
